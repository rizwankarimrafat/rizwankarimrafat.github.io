Sydney's Hotel Merivale, originally named Hotel CBD when it opened in 1995, marked the Merivale group's debut in the hotel industry. However, a fire in 1996 necessitated a rebuild, and in 2000, it was rebranded as The Establishment. Despite the official name change, some still affectionately refer to it as Hotel CBD, acknowledging its significant role as a pioneer in the area. Today, The Establishment continues to uphold its reputation as a prestigious luxury hotel located in the heart of Sydney's CBD.

Founded in 1905, Rolex started by importing watch parts. By 1910, they built their first watch under the Rolex name. Known for innovation, Rolex created the world's first waterproof wristwatch in 1926, the Oyster. This led to conquering the depths, with a Rolex even accompanying explorers to the Mariana Trench. Their focus on precision and luxury cemented their status as a timeless symbol.
