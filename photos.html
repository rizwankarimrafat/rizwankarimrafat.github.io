Sydney, NSW, Australia

08 January 2024


The Louis Vuitton store on George Street in Sydney, established in 1986, has been a significant player in the city's luxury landscape. In 2011, it moved to a historic building and became the Louis Vuitton George Street Maison, offering unique personalization services. After a recent renovation that blended heritage with modern design, the Maison continues to be a Sydney landmark for luxury shopping.

Sydney, NSW, Australia

08 January 2024


Sydney's Hotel Merivale, initially Hotel CBD (1995), marked the Merivale group's first foray into hotels. A 1996 fire led to a rebuild and 2000 name change to The Establishment. While officially renamed, some still call it Hotel CBD, a nod to its pioneering role. Today, The Establishment remains a renowned Sydney CBD luxury hotel.
